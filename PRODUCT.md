# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students and recruiters viewing a personal portfolio app. Primary context: mobile device browsing a student's project showcase and contact form.

## Product Purpose

Personal portfolio app for GianneFran, an Informatics Engineering student at UNETI, Venezuela. Showcases academic profile, tech stack, projects, and contact channels. Serves as a professional presence for job opportunities and academic evaluations.

## Brand Personality

Modern, dark, confident. The Deep Navy + Violet glassmorphism aesthetic communicates technical sophistication without being pretentious. Voice is approachable but professional.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS cream/sand/white templates
- Overly corporate blue-and-white designs
- Cluttered portfolio layouts with too many sections
- Bright/neon color schemes that feel juvenile

## Design Principles

1. Dark-first: Deep Navy as the constant canvas, violet for emphasis
2. Glassmorphism as identity: frosted glass cards create depth without heaviness
3. Minimal chrome: let content breathe, avoid decorative borders and ornaments
4. Touch-friendly: generous tap targets, clear interactive feedback

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Basic WCAG AA contrast on text (verified in code)
- Semantic HTML structure
- Menu navigation with proper ARIA attributes
